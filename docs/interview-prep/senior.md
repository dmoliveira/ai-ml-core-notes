# Senior Interview Track

Use this track for senior/principal AI engineering and technical leadership roles.

## What interviewers look for

- End-to-end architecture judgment under constraints
- Robust reliability and security posture for AI systems
- Clear strategy for team velocity, quality, and operational excellence
- Strong narrative on roadmap impact and measurable business outcomes

## Study checklist

1. Propose a resilient AI platform architecture with failure modes
2. Define model and prompt governance with policy controls
3. Design multi-tenant observability and cost guardrails
4. Present a 90-day AI roadmap with milestones and risks

## Mini practice

- Scenario: migrate a prototype assistant into a production platform
- Include: SLOs, rollback strategy, on-call and incident triage flow
- Deliverable: 2-page design doc + executive summary

## Common mistakes to avoid

- Over-indexing on tooling and under-specifying operating model.
- Skipping cost controls and multi-team governance boundaries.
- Presenting roadmap outcomes without measurable leading indicators.
